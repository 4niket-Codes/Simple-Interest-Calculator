# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Positive behavior includes:

* Respectful communication
* Accepting constructive feedback
* Supporting other contributors

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, or offensive language.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces and interactions related to this project.

## Enforcement

Reports of unacceptable behavior may be submitted to the project maintainers and will be reviewed promptly.

## Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers may issue warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on the severity of violations.
